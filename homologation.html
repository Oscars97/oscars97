HOMOLOGATION

Foreigners living in Costa Rica who wish to drive vehicles in the country may opt for the homologation of the foreign driver's license. It is important to keep in mind that people who have a foreign license can drive the vehicles allowed by the category obtained within 3 months after the last entry into the country. Through this process, you can obtain the license you have, either from your country of origin or from a third party.
